## 2. Mission, Vision & Core Values

- **Mission:** "TO PRODUCE AN INTEGRATED, EFFECTIVE AND A BALANCED CHILD WHO IS ACADEMICALLY AND RELIGIOUSLY SOUND."
- **Vision:** "A PLACE WHERE ANY CHILD CAN BE TRANSFORMED INTO A PRODUCTIVE CITIZEN ANYWHERE IN THE WORLD"
- **Motto:** "SEEK KNOWLEDGE AND ATTAIN WISDOM"

### Commitment
Lukman Primary School (LPS) is committed to:
- Provide a safe and orderly environment conducive to learning
- Meet the expectations of the parents and pupils
- Provide pupils the learning opportunities with the highest standards of education
- Commensurate with Ministry of Education and the country's guidelines
- Involve all stakeholders in school activities and programs

## 3. Why the Name "Lukman"
Named after **Lukman Ibn Anqa Ibn Sadun/Ibn Tharan** - a pious man mentioned in the Holy Quran Chapter 31 (Surat Lukman). He was known for:
- An Ethiopian slave/carpenter, or a man from Black men of Egypt
- Given wisdom (not prophethood)
- A judge during the lifetime of Prophet David
- Known for telling truth, discarding what doesn't concern him, and choosing wisdom
